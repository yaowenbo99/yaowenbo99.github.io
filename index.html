About Me

I am currently pursuing a Ph.D. in the Department of Computing at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU), under the supervision of Prof. Ken Yiu. I completed my M.S. degree in Electronic and Information Engineering at PolyU in 2023. Prior to this, I obtained dual bachelor’s degrees from the Adelaide University, majoring in Electrical and Electronic Engineering, graduating with Second Upper Class Honours (H2A) in July 2021. I also had the opportunity to visit the University of Reading in 2018.

I previously worked for China Telecom as a Backend Developer and Government-Enterprise Solutions Architect under Talent Scheme (优才计划). I also had the opportunity to intern at Huawei and Insta360, where I contributed to the application of the IEEE 1588 V2 protocol and speech enhancement algorithms, respectively.

My research interests lie in databases, with a particular focus on data visualization and query optimization.
