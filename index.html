Biography

I am a PhD student in the Department of Computing at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU), under the supervision of Prof. Ken Yiu. I completed my master degree in Electronic and Information Engineering (EIE) at PolyU in 2023. Prior to this, I obtained dual bachelor’s degrees from the Adelaide University (UniSA), majoring in Electrical and Electronic Engineering, graduating with Second Upper Class Honours (H2A) in July 2021. I also had the opportunity to visit the University of Reading in 2018.

I previously worked for China Telecom as a Backend Developer and Government-Enterprise Solutions Architect under Talent Scheme (优才计划). I also had the opportunity to intern at Huawei and Insta360, where I contributed to the application of the IEEE 1588 V2 protocol and speech enhancement algorithms, respectively.

Research Interests

My research interests lie in databases, with a particular focus on data visualization and query optimization for large-scale and complex data. If you’re interested in solving efficient programming problems or exploring potential collaborations, feel free to drop me an email.
